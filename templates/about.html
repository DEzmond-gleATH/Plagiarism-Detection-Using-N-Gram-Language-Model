About

Plagiarism is a serious issue in academic and professional writing, and detecting it is essential to maintain the integrity of written work. This website presents a plagiarism detection method using the n-gram language model. The proposed method compares the n-gram language model of the suspected document with the n-gram models of a set of reference documents to identify similarities and potential plagiarism. The n-gram language model is a statistical language model that predicts the probability of the next word in a sequence based on the previous n-1 words. In this method, we use the n-gram language model to represent the textual content of documents, and compare their n-gram models to identify similarities. The proposed method has been evaluated on a dataset of academic papers and achieved high accuracy in detecting plagiarism. The results show that the method can effectively detect various types of plagiarism, including copy-pasting, paraphrasing, and sentence-level plagiarism. Overall, the proposed plagiarism detection method using the n-gram language model can be a useful tool for educators, researchers, and professionals to maintain the integrity of written work and prevent plagiarism.
